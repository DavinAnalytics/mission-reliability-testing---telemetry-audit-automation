This case study documents the deployment of an in-memory transformation pipeline designed to ingest messy, high-frequency autonomous drone sensor logs, sanitize structural timing anomalies, and expose critical hardware failure signatures to Field Operations teams.

1. Attitude Control Drift Failure

Occurs when actual sensor tracking orientation systematically deviates from the commanded flight path. Flagged when ErrRP consistently exceeds baseline limits.

2. Critical Oscillation & Structural Shock

High-frequency vibrations or severe landing impacts isolated via Euclidean 3D Vector Magnitudes. Spikes in total_kinetic_shock_g pinpoint structural risk events.
